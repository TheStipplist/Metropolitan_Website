THE WORLD AUTHOR START READING

The gilded age has ended long ago. Once gleaming gold and immaculate imaginings now finally chipped, having falling to smashing to the ground. A land where comfort and safety wrapped everyone nice and tight, pulled to far at the seams.

An ark of humanity with all the living bizarrely marooned despite on land. Their craft now hoisted high! Above them like lamb on the bob them bobbing atop the ground's puddle. They've climbed!

Possibly with the same velocity that first got them there. Who's gonna get there first? They may have all their glory rusted away, it wasn't the wind or sand.

SONNY!

An enigma to both himself and all those around him whether "Discovered" or "rescued" by Cheryl, Sonny travels Cheryl's world alongside her in his quest to find out what ended his own.

Is he a failed hero, or was he a successful destroyer, and is there even a difference between the two?

CHERYL!

A violent, aggressive young woman with crimson hair has only one goal; answers.

A life with nothing but unresolved questions Cheryl goes forth. Her strength is inhuman, and knows not "why?" The land she lives in both chaotic and wondrous means nothing to her without "how?"

When she comes across Sonny she still has both and now a third "who?" or even a fourth; "what?" Her answers she knows "where"... above, nothing will stop her.

OLIVER!

A showman that travels the land carrying infamy and intrigue. In his hand a camera that he claims will capture the very climax of the world. While some call his strength feeble no one says the same about his mouth.

Alongside his brother they bestow gifts to those they deem fit. Sometimes this can cause quite the commotion.

MOLOCH!

Leader of the only organized military group to still be around, Moloch with his men and his girls hunt for tech. No one believes they have an actual goal in mind besides the old medieval logic of "we take because we can".

Any piece of technology they can use to repair their own or modify for combat they make theirs. If that piece is attached to someone, they join up. If the owner says "no" theres a bit of do a bit of "surgury" Then move on their way.

THE RICHARD!

Tall and quiet "The Richard" stands looming beside his brother Oliver. Riding the wave of his brother's mysterious journey he possesses forces all his own appearing almost limitless.

he's toting a tote at his waist. Inside weapons and devices that could maybe shatter armies, or just simply shatter. Gifted marksmen, talented engineer, terrible conversationalist.

I really love Dieselpunk, or Decopunk as some call it. It's a big part of why I write and draw these stories. If you look at these subjects, despite the clear flaws and issues of the time. They represent something incredible.

An Idea of achieving goals and accomplishments that smash the reason of the world circumstances. The same could be said of most great works. Except, there was an imagination with a bright simplicity unseen since early myth.

They imagined men and women who flew and had strength unattainable. Those men and women fought creatures much like us but so purely conjectural to common good. At the same time in reality a dark noir all people knew and acted away was everywhere.
